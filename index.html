Three things, in order

  1. Press ⌃⌥C in any app. The shortcut is re-mappable. ⌃⌥P opens your recent pins.

A link can be so much more

A pin carries the detail that makes it worth pasting — the timestamp, the page, the exact sentence you selected.

?t=102

YouTube, with the playhead

Clean youtu.be short links. Opt in per browser, and a paused video's link carries the timestamp — and the channel's name.

#:~:text=

Highlight links

Select text on any page and the link reopens to that exact passage. Copy more highlights on the same page and they merge into one link.

#page=4

PDFs open at your page

Preview and Skim links carry the page you were reading. Works in any browser's PDF viewer; Skim links round-trip perfectly.

ssh://

Knows when you're remote

Terminals give the working directory. SSH'd into a server? You get ssh://you@host instead — parsed from the real ssh process.

gh repo clone

GitHub-aware

A github.com pin also offers the owner/repo slug, HTTPS and SSH clone URLs, the gh command, and raw-file URLs.

slack://

That message, exactly

In Slack, Pinza copies a link to the selected message — the archive URL, or a slack:// deep-link that opens the app.

things:///

Things, kept live

A Things pin knows its to-do's status. Complete, cancel, or reopen it from Pinza — and see when it's been deleted.

3 files

Real files, plural

Select three files in Finder and the copy pastes as three actual files — into Mail, Slack, or another Finder window.

⌘Z

Rename from the card

Just copied a file? Rename it right on the confirmation card — with an undo chip, and a warning if you change the extension.

this evening

Remind me

Any pin becomes an Apple Reminder: in an hour, this evening, tomorrow morning, or a time you pick. Write-only — Pinza never reads your reminders.

⇧⌘D

Cards

Pop a pin out of the list as a floating note. Keep up to fifteen on screen; they survive a relaunch.

1–9

While the confirmation card is up, the number keys pick a format. No mouse, no menu diving.

Notion links, cleaned

A Notion pin drops the tracking parameter before it reaches your clipboard. Clean links only.

FAQ

Is there a subscription?

No. Pinza Pro is $9.99, once. Every 1.x update is free. If a 2.0 ever ships as a paid upgrade, 1.x customers get 50% off.

How many Macs does one license cover?

Five. Deactivate a Mac from Settings → Pro to free a seat.

What stays free?

Everything that makes Pinza Pinza: all app integrations, both shortcuts, every Copy as… format, the account integrations, and the recents popover with your last 25 items. The free tier is not a trial.

Why does Pinza ask for the Accessibility permission?

Reading the frontmost app's state — the tab URL, the selected file — uses macOS accessibility APIs and AppleScript. That's the entire use: read on shortcut press, never in the background. The privacy policy has the details.

What's the MCP server?

MCP is the open protocol AI apps use to talk to local tools. Pinza Pro's MCP server lets your AI agent — Claude, Codex, Gemini, or any MCP client — search and read your pins locally, over a Unix socket, with nothing leaving your Mac. You paste one snippet into the agent's config and it works.

Which MCP commands are there?

Five, each with its own toggle in Settings:

  • search_pins — full-text search across your pins
  • list_pins — list pins, filtered by app, site, or date
  • get_pin — one pin's details, including its highlights
  • reveal_pin — pop a pin up on screen as a floating Card
  • copy_pin — put a pin back on your clipboard. The only tool that can write, so it ships turned off.
Apple Silicon or Intel?

Pinza is a universal binary and runs natively on both. It needs macOS 14 Sonoma or newer.

Where is my data?

On your Mac, in an encrypted SQLite database under ~/Library/Application Support/Pinza/. The encryption key lives in your Keychain and never leaves the device.

What's the refund policy?

14 days, no questions asked — through Gumroad or by emailing hello@pinza.app.

Why isn't Pinza in the Mac App Store?

The App Store sandbox forbids apps that read other apps' state — which is Pinza's entire job. Every comparable utility (Alfred, Keyboard Maestro, Bartender, Hookmark) distributes directly for the same reason. Pinza is notarized by Apple and updates itself securely via Sparkle.
